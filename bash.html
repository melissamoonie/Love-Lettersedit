Love Letter to Git Love Letter to Git Hub Love Letter to Bash

Dear Bash,
I feel so bashful just thinking about you. How could I even spend a nanosecond without you in my life? Without that nano, how could I edit my websites? You really know how to touch me and we create so many great files together. If I ever get lost, I never hesitate to command pwd and then I quickly find myself again. You helped me come out of my shell and now I feel comfortable in your shell. To be honest, sometimes you make me want to bash my computer to pieces and break up with you but I always forgive you in the end. I know that you care about me and I care about you too.
P.S. Don't tell Git about our secret love affair.
